More about opportunityTank

The primary purpose of OpportunityTank is to serve as a catalyst for job creation and employment opportunities for young people in Rwanda and across Africa. By providing a centralized platform where users can showcase their skills, connect with potential employers, and access job preparation resources, OpportunityTank aims to reduce unemployment and underemployment among the youth. This platform aims to bridge the gap between skilled young individuals and employers worldwide, facilitating connections that can lead to job placements. Additionally, OpportunityTank will offer resources and tools to help users prepare for the job market, enhancing their employability and career prospects.
