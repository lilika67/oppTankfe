About the Company

The primary purpose of OpportunityTank is to serve as a catalyst for job creation and employment opportunities for young people in Rwanda and across Africa. By providing a centralized platform where users can showcase their skills, connect with potential employers, and access job preparation resources, OpportunityTank aims to reduce unemployment and underemployment among the youth

This platform aims to bridge the gap between skilled young individuals and employers worldwide, facilitating connections that can lead to job placements. Additionally, OpportunityTank will offer resources and tools to help users prepare for the job market, enhancing their employability and career prospects.

Why OpportunityTank?

Easy job connections

OpportunityTank connects young job seekers with employers from around the globe, increasing their chances of securing meaningful employment.

Global reach

We enable Rwandan and African youth to access job markets beyond their local regions, opening up a wider array of career opportunities.
